## Installation

### Prerequisites

- Processwire version 3.0.255 or higher

### Steps

You can install this module directly from the ProcessWire admin panel:

1. Log in to the ProcessWire Admin.

2. Go to Modules → New.

3. Scroll down to Add Module From Upload.

4. Click Choose File and select the module ZIP file.

5. Click Upload.

6. After the upload completes, ProcessWire will detect the module.

7. Click Install to complete the installation.

| `https://www.skynettechnologies.com` | Skynet Technologies USA LLC | Hosts product documentation, support resources, accessibility guides, and related services. |

During activation and normal operation, limited website and configuration information is securely transmitted to Skynet Technologies USA LLC in order to configure, license, and deliver the accessibility features for your website. A dedicated EU infrastructure is available for eligible customers where applicable.
 
### What data is transmitted, and why
 
| Data | When it's sent | Why it's needed |
|---|---|---|
| Website domain (URL) | During activation and widget configuration | Associates the widget license and configuration with your website. |
| Widget configuration (color, position, size, language, enabled features, and other display settings) | When widget settings are saved or updated | Applies your selected accessibility widget configuration. |
| License and activation information | During activation and license validation | Verifies the subscription status and enables the appropriate widget features. |
| Account information (such as name, email address, phone number, company, and billing details, when applicable) | During account registration, subscription, or billing | Creates and manages your account, licensing, subscriptions, and customer support. |
